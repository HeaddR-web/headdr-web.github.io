Privacy Policy — BeThatHost

Last updated: 16 June 2026

BeThatHost ("we") publishes hosting and party-planning guides across our occasion sites (BeThatHost, Watch Party HQ and more). We also run a small tool that publishes our own pins to our own Pinterest account through the official Pinterest API. This policy explains what data we handle.

The Pinterest publishing tool

The tool reads pre-prepared image and text content and creates pins on a Pinterest board that we own. It is used for our own marketing only and does not act on behalf of, or collect data from, any other Pinterest users.

  • It uses a Pinterest access token for our own account, obtained via Pinterest's standard OAuth consent. The token is stored privately and is used solely to publish our pins.
  • It uses our own board identifier, so pins land on the correct board.

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data of any third parties through this tool.

Advertising

Our sites may display ads served by third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can manage ad personalisation through your browser settings and the ad network's own controls.

Affiliate links

Some links on our sites are affiliate links (for example, Amazon Associates). If you buy something through one, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. A commission never changes our recommendation.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to a newsletter, we store the email address you give us solely to send that newsletter, via our email provider. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

Third-party services

Our tools communicate with Pinterest; your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy. Advertising is governed by the relevant ad network's policies (e.g. Google).

Data retention & revocation

The Pinterest access token can be revoked at any time from the Pinterest account settings, which immediately stops the tool's access. Stored credentials can be deleted at any time.

Contact

For any privacy question, reach us through the contact details listed on our sites.
