About Girls Night HQ

Girls Night HQ is your one-stop planner for the perfect night in with your favourite people. We help you pull a whole evening together — games, drinks, snacks, decor, pamper rituals and the right movie — and turn your picks into a single, ready-to-shop list. We write the kind of guides we'd actually use to host.

How the night-builder works

Your "My Night" list is stored only in your own browser (using localStorage) so it's there when you come back. We never send it to a server, and clearing your browser data removes it.

Affiliate disclosure

Some links on Girls Night HQ are affiliate links. If you buy something through one of them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only link to things that fit the idea we're writing about, and a commission never changes our recommendation. Affiliate links are marked with a "Shop" / "Check price" button and carry a rel="sponsored" tag.

As an Amazon Associate (and member of other affiliate programs), Girls Night HQ earns from qualifying purchases.

Advertising

Girls Night HQ may display ads served by third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can manage ad personalization through your browser settings and the ad network's own controls.

Privacy

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store the email address you give us solely to send the newsletter, via our email provider. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email, which deletes you from the list. We don't sell or share your email with anyone.

We use privacy-respecting traffic measurement to understand which guides are useful. We don't try to identify individual visitors.
